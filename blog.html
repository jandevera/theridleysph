A Mall Gig

Feb 11 by Admin

You won't believe who came to watch at our mall gig!

This is probably the most awkward gig that we had. We were playing inside the mall, but no one was listening to us. Maybe, some people stayed and listened for a bit, but left after a song or two. It was hard because in a bar setting (ex, Route 196), people came to see you. But in the mall, people came to shop. We have another gig at SM Cherry Shaw. Maybe things will be a little bit different.

First gig of the year!

Feb 10 by Admin

You won't believe what happened during our set!

During DYWTBM and Raconteur, Jan's effects pedals lost power during the instrumental parts of both songs. Imagine, all the focus is on him, but there was no guitar sound. Good thing the other members were quick in 'taking the spotlight from him' while he fixed his equipment.

Guess who's back?

Jan 21 by Admin

You won't believe who just got back from vacation!

This post will be a little reflection. We'll look at 2017, and we'll remember all the blessings and the lessons that we got.

2017 was our big year. Last September 9, we had our launch for our EP, 'From Morning Light 'till Break of Dawn', at Route 196! It was a memorable night!

Also, this year, we have decided to keep track of all our events, all our thoughts, and we'll place them in this little blog of ours

Anyway... To answer our questions, guess who's back? Our lead guitarist, Jan de Vera, just arrived here in Manila. He is from Cebu. He usually goes home to spend the holidays with his family.
